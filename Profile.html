Points Betting Overview

Points betting is putting money behind an outcome of your choice and getting paid if that outcome is achieved

Betting odds show how likely an outcome is and how much points you can win from a bet. Understanding these odds helps beginners know the risk of a bet and estimate how much they could earn if their prediction is correct.


What are Odds?

Understanding the odds helps bettors make better decisions and avoid making hasty bets.

Negative Odds (-) = shows how much points you want to bet to win points.

Example: Odds of -200 mean you must bet 200 points to win 100 points. If you win, you get a total of 300 points (200 stake + 100 profit)

Positive Odds (+) = shows how much profit you can make from how much points you betted.

Example: Odds of +150 means if you bet 100 points, you can win 150 points. If you win, you get a total of 250 points (200 stake + 150 profit)
